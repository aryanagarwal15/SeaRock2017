Registration

There Are 4 Rounds To Win Searock Online Elimination: Each band needs to submit a link of their original work which will be used in shortlisting 15 bands for regional eliminations. City Elimination: 15 bands will compete in regional eliminations out of which 2 will be shortlisted for Semi-Finals in goa. Semi-Finals: Regional winners will battle it down in the semis at Waves'17. 3 bands will be shortlisted for finale. Finale: These 3 bands will open for the headlining act. This round will be the final one judging them for 1st, 2nd and 3rd position.
